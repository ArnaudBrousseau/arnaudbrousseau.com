Georges Duquin is a French author and painter.
I met him as a complete anonymous stranger in the train one day, and we ended up talking about art, literature...and websites. He needed a new one, so I decided to take on this project.
I took advantage of it to learn 3 new things: how to use Twitter Bootstrap, how to use Perch (a very tiny CMS), and how to resist the urge to fire Photoshop when designing web interfaces. That's right, this project was designed in the browser from the beginning to the end.

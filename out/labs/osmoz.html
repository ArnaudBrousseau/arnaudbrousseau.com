Student Government Office: Osmoz

This website is the first real-world project I’ve been working on. The BDE (Student Government Office) of my engineering school needed a webmaster, and a cool website to be able to put some information online.
I built this website from scratch : design, code, interactions,...
That was an amazing experience. The most interesting part about this website was the feedback from users: more than 800 students were using the site for almost 2 years.
